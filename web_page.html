Mahendra Singh Dhoni

About MS Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, fondly known as "Captain Cool", is one of the greatest cricket captains in history. Born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, India, Dhoni led India to victory in all three major ICC tournaments: the T20 World Cup in 2007, the ODI World Cup in 2011, and the Champions Trophy in 2013. Known for his calm demeanor and exceptional finishing skills, Dhoni also brought multiple IPL titles to Chennai Super Kings as captain.
